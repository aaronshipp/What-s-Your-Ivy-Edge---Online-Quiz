What's Your Ivy Edge?

A personal communication style assessment that reveals how you think, connect, and lead — and how to use it to your advantage.

How It Works

  1. You'll complete 10 rounds, each with 8 words or statements.
  2. In each round, drag and drop the items to rank them — most like you at the top, least like you at the bottom.
  3. There are no right or wrong answers. Go with your gut — your first instinct is usually your truest one.
  4. At the end, you'll receive a detailed profile of your personal communication style and what makes you unique.
